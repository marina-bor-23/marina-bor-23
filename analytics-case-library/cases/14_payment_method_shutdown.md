# Payment Method Shutdown Impact Analysis

## Role Targets
Payments Analyst, Data Analyst, Product Analyst

## Business Question
What would be the user, transaction and revenue risk of disabling two payment methods in one market?

## Stack
Python, pandas, NumPy, BigQuery, openpyxl, user-overlap analysis, weekly trend analysis.

## Metrics
User loss, transaction loss, deposit volume loss, method overlap, migration rate, risk group without alternative methods, weekly usage trend.

## What I Did
- Analyzed 758K transactions and 20K users across six months.
- Identified target payment methods and cleaned method-agent combinations.
- Measured total volume, user count, average check and median check for target methods.
- Calculated user overlap between methods and alternative-method usage.
- Built risk groups for users who used only the target methods.

## Business Impact
Quantified direct losses and migration risk before a payment-method shutdown decision, giving the payments team a clear view of users likely to be lost.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Assessed payment method shutdown risk using 758K transactions and 20K users.
- Calculated user overlap, migration potential and users without alternative payment behavior.
- Built weekly trend and loss analysis to support payment product decisions.
