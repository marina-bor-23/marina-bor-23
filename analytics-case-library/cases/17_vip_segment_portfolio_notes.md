# VIP and Segment Portfolio Notes

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, CRM Analyst, Data Analyst

## Business Question
How can VIP and non-VIP segments be monitored to identify retention opportunities, margin risks and underused growth potential?

## Stack
SQL, Python, BigQuery, pandas, Excel reporting, segmentation analysis.

## Metrics
VIP deposits, active users, ARPU, retention, LTV, NGR, margin, segment share, conversion from first deposit to later activity.

## What I Did
- Compared VIP and non-VIP users across lifecycle, value and retention metrics.
- Identified segments with high ARPU but weak long-term retention.
- Highlighted margin risks in specific VIP groups where bonuses reduced profitability.
- Prepared segment-level recommendations for CRM and product teams.

## Business Impact
Supported better prioritization of CRM effort: protect valuable VIP users, improve early activation and avoid over-incentivizing unprofitable segments.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built VIP and non-VIP segment analysis across retention, LTV, ARPU and profitability metrics.
- Identified high-value segments with weak retention and segments with negative margin risk.
- Delivered CRM and product recommendations for early lifecycle retention and bonus policy.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for CRM, retention, VIP analytics, lifecycle strategy and product segmentation.

## Anonymization Notes
VIP tier names, bonus mechanics and financial values are generalized.
