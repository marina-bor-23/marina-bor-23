# Wager Change Impact Analysis

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Data Analyst, Risk Analyst

## Business Question
How did a change in withdrawal wager requirements affect deposits, bets, withdrawals, GGR, churn and retention?

## Stack
Python, BigQuery, pandas, Excel reporting, segmentation analysis, statistical comparison.

## Metrics
Deposit count, bets count, withdrawal sum, withdrawal percentage, GGR, retention rate, churn rate.

## What I Did
- Compared user behavior before and after the wager policy change.
- Segmented users by GEO, VIP segment, traffic source and bet type.
- Calculated retention and churn definitions based on deposit and betting activity.
- Analyzed impact on GGR and withdrawal behavior across user groups.

## Business Impact
Helped evaluate whether the policy change improved monetization without damaging retention in key segments.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Analyzed the impact of a wager policy change on GGR, withdrawals, retention and churn.
- Built multi-dimensional segmentation across GEO, VIP status, traffic source and bet type.
- Identified segments where monetization improved and churn risk remained controlled.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for product policy, risk, monetization and gaming analytics.
