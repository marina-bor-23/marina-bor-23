# Cash Desk Outage Impact Analysis

## Role Targets
Payments Analyst, Product Analyst, Data Analyst

## Business Question
How did withdrawal errors affect player activity, monetization and value across traffic types and VIP segments?

## Stack
Python, BigQuery, pandas, Excel reporting, segmentation analysis.

## Metrics
Failed withdrawals, GGR, NGR, net profit, active users, user value with and without withdrawal errors, VIP and traffic-source breakdowns.

## What I Did
- Extracted failed withdrawal transactions for the analysis period.
- Compared players affected by errors with unaffected players.
- Analyzed revenue and activity impact by traffic type, VIP segment and user cohort.
- Highlighted user groups where payment errors were connected with negative value or reduced activity.

## Business Impact
Helped quantify the financial and behavioral impact of payment failures and gave payments/product teams a prioritized view of affected segments.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Assessed the impact of withdrawal errors on player value, GGR, NGR and activity.
- Built segmentation by traffic source, VIP status and affected/unaffected player groups.
- Delivered a business impact view for payment outage prioritization.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for payments analytics, product health monitoring and operational impact analysis.

## Anonymization Notes
Payment system names, GEO and exact failure causes are generalized.
