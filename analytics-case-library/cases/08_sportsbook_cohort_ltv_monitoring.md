# Sportsbook Cohort and LTV Monitoring

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Data Analyst, Sportsbook Analyst

## Business Question
How do player cohorts perform over time in deposits, NGR and payer activity, and which lifecycle windows require attention?

## Stack
Python, BigQuery, pandas, Excel reporting, cohort analysis.

## Metrics
Deposits at N days, NGR at N days, payers at N days, total deposits, total NGR, total payers.

## What I Did
- Combined deposit data from different source periods into one analytical base.
- Built lifecycle windows such as 0, 30, 60, 90, 120, 150, 180, 270 days and total.
- Calculated deposits, NGR and payer counts by lifecycle window.
- Prepared cohort-style monitoring for sportsbook performance.

## Business Impact
Created a reusable view for understanding player value development over time and comparing cohort quality.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built cohort monitoring logic for sportsbook users across multiple lifecycle windows.
- Calculated deposits, NGR and payer activity at 0-270 day intervals and total lifetime.
- Unified historical and current BigQuery sources into a single analytical base.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for sportsbook analytics, cohort analytics, LTV monitoring and lifecycle analysis.
