# Registration Funnel Analysis

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Growth Analyst, Data Analyst

## Business Question
How did GEO-sensitive registration form customization affect conversion, error rate, drop-off rate and form completion time across countries and devices?

## Stack
Python, pandas, NumPy, BigQuery, GA4 event data, openpyxl, funnel analysis.

## Metrics
Registration conversion, error rate, leave rate, completion time, event counts, device split, registration type performance.

## What I Did
- Processed 225K registration events and 87K unique users.
- Built a 20-step funnel from registration page open to success, error or leave.
- Calculated conversion, error and drop-off metrics by country, device and registration type.
- Measured form completion time by session and removed outliers.
- Generated an Excel report with funnel, country, device, registration type and timing views.

## Business Impact
Provided a detailed view of where registration friction remained after form customization and which countries/devices needed product attention.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built a 20-step registration funnel from GA4 events across 12 countries and desktop/mobile/app traffic.
- Measured conversion, errors, drop-offs and completion time by GEO, device and registration type.
- Automated a 20+ sheet Excel report for product and UX decision-making.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for product funnel, growth, activation, GA4, UX analytics and conversion optimization.
