# Weekly KPI Automation Pipeline

## Role Targets
Data Analyst, BI Analyst, Product Analyst

## Business Question
How can weekly sportsbook performance reporting be automated across FTD, GGR, NGR, deposits, GEOs, traffic types and VIP segments?

## Stack
SQL, Python, BigQuery, pandas, Excel reporting.

## Metrics
FTD count and amount, GGR, NGR, deposits, active users, VIP deposits, VIP active share, country and traffic-source breakdowns.

- Aggregated core product and finance metrics by week, country, traffic source and bonus choice.
- Added VIP and non-VIP breakdowns for product monitoring.
- Prepared a report structure for regular stakeholder use.

## Business Impact
Reduced manual reporting effort and created a consistent weekly view of product performance for operational and business teams.
