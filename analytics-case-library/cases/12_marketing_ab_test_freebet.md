# Marketing Campaign A/B Test: Freebet vs No Freebet

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, CRM Analyst, Growth Analyst

## Business Question
Did a freebet communication campaign improve post-campaign deposits, bets, NGR and retention compared with non-freebet groups?

## Stack
Python, BigQuery, pandas, statistical testing, Excel reporting.

## Metrics
Deposits, bets, NGR, conversion, RTP, VIP segment, post-campaign retention, group-level activity.

## What I Did
- Compared multiple campaign groups with different offer logic.
- Calculated user activity and financial metrics for each group.
- Added segmentation by GEO and VIP status.
- Prepared a test-style comparison for post-campaign behavior.

## Business Impact
Helped retention and marketing teams understand whether freebet mechanics drove profitable user activity or mainly increased cost.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Evaluated a multi-group marketing campaign comparing freebet and non-freebet communication strategies.
- Measured post-campaign deposits, bets, NGR, conversion and retention by group and GEO.
- Produced recommendations for future campaign targeting and offer design.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for A/B testing, CRM analytics, campaign analytics and growth analysis.
