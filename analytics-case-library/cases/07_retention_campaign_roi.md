# Retention Campaign ROI Analysis

## Role Targets
CRM Analyst, Product Analyst, Data Analyst

## Business Question
Did a retention campaign reactivate churned players profitably, and which offers, GEOs and user groups produced the strongest post-campaign behavior?

## Stack
Python, pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, BigQuery, CSV parsing, Excel files, openpyxl.

## Metrics
Bonus cost, deposits after campaign, withdrawals after campaign, profit after campaign, net effect, active days after bonus, D1/D3/D7/D11/D16/D21/D30 retention.

## What I Did
- Parsed more than 250 offer CSV files and multiple promo Excel files.
- Matched campaign offers with user activity and financial behavior.
- Calculated net campaign effect at user level.
- Built retention curves by GEO, offer and user segment.
- Compared behavior of users who used the bonus with users who did not.

## Business Impact
Identified which offers produced profitable reactivation and which groups had weak post-bonus retention, supporting better targeting and offer design.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built a retention campaign ROI analysis combining offer files, promo data and user activity from BigQuery.
- Calculated user-level bonus cost, post-campaign profit and net effect.
- Measured D1-D30 retention by GEO and offer type to support CRM optimization.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for CRM, retention, lifecycle marketing and campaign ROI analysis.
