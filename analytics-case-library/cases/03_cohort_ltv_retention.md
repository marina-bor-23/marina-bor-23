# Cohort-Based Business Health Audit

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Data Analyst, Growth Analyst

## Business Question
Why did revenue growth slow down despite growth in the paying user base, and which cohorts, segments and GEOs were profitable or risky?

## Stack
Python, pandas, NumPy, SciPy, BigQuery, Parquet, XlsxWriter, cohort analysis, retention analysis, LTV analysis.

## Metrics
Registration to FTD funnel, paying users, M1/M3/M6/M12 retention, cumulative LTV, NGR LTV, ARPU, margin, LTV to CPA ratio.

## What I Did
- Loaded and optimized 23 months of raw product and revenue data.
- Built semi-monthly cohorts for granular lifecycle tracking.
- Automated metric calculation by player type, VIP segment and top GEOs.
- Decomposed revenue decline into acquisition quality, retention degradation and base effects.
- Generated a structured Excel report for business stakeholders.

## Business Impact
Showed that user volume was growing while new-user ARPU and retention quality were declining. Identified that 70-80% of LTV was generated in the first 90 days, which supported recommendations around onboarding, bonus policy and early retention.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built a cohort and LTV audit across 23 months of product data using Python and BigQuery.
- Calculated M1/M3/M6/M12 retention, ARPU, NGR LTV and segment profitability.
- Identified early lifecycle retention as the main lever, with 70-80% of LTV generated in the first 90 days.

Relevant for product analytics, growth analytics, retention analytics and business health diagnostics.
