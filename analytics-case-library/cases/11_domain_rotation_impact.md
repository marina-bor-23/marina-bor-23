# Domain Rotation Impact Analysis

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Data Analyst, Growth Analyst

## Business Question
How did domain rotation and access blockages affect deposits, registrations, new-user activation and retention in one market?

## Stack
Python, BigQuery, pandas, natural experiment analysis, Excel reporting.

## Metrics
New users, deposits, D1/D3/D7 retention, time to first deposit, active users, period-over-period change.

## What I Did
- Treated domain rotation as a natural experiment across time periods.
- Compared activity before, during and after blockage-related changes.
- Focused separately on existing users and newly registered users.
- Measured whether new-user growth came with weaker retention quality.

## Business Impact
Separated access-related effects from potential seasonality and highlighted that new-user retention needed separate investigation.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Analyzed domain rotation as a natural experiment affecting user activity and retention.
- Compared new-user acquisition, D1/D3/D7 retention and time to first deposit across periods.
- Identified that growth in new users did not necessarily translate into stronger retention.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for product health, market access, growth diagnostics and natural experiment analysis.

## Anonymization Notes
Market, domain details and exact percentage changes are generalized.
