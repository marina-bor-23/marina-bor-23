# Winners vs Losers Strategic Analysis

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Data Analyst, Strategy Analyst

## Business Question
What is the long-term value of winning players versus losing players, and how should the business treat these groups from a retention and profitability perspective?

## Stack
Python, pandas, SciPy, BigQuery, regression, cohort retention, visualization.

## Metrics
LTV, total profit, average profit, median profit, profit margin, lifetime days, deposit frequency, repeat deposit rate, churn rate, bonus conversion rate, retention at 1/3/6/12 months, payback period.

## What I Did
- Compared winning and losing player groups across monetization, activity and retention.
- Added segmentation by VIP status, GEO, traffic source, game category, registration source and win size.
- Used t-tests, correlation analysis and regression for LTV forecasting.
- Built cohort retention heatmaps and visual summaries.

## Business Impact
Produced a strategic view of whether winning users can still be valuable long-term and which segments should be retained, monitored or deprioritized.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Built a strategic LTV analysis comparing winning and losing player cohorts across retention, churn and profitability.
- Applied statistical tests, correlation analysis and regression to forecast long-term value.
- Produced multi-segment recommendations by VIP status, traffic source and player behavior.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for strategy analytics, LTV modeling, executive analytics and product monetization.
