# Minimum Deposit Increase Impact Analysis

## Role Targets
Product Analyst, Payments Analyst, Data Analyst

## Business Question
How would increasing the minimum deposit threshold affect revenue, transactions, user churn and the new-player funnel?

## Stack
Python, pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, BigQuery, openpyxl, distribution analysis, first-deposit cohort analysis, LTV analysis.

## Metrics
Revenue loss, transaction loss, user loss, small-deposit share, first-deposit category, repeat deposit rate, LTV by first-deposit category.

## What I Did
- Analyzed 389K successful deposits across six months.
- Converted multicurrency deposits into comparable USD logic.
- Segmented users into small-only, mixed and large-deposit groups.
- Measured losses by payment method, traffic source, player type and VIP segment.
- Analyzed first-deposit size and later LTV behavior.

## Business Impact
Showed the tradeoff between increasing minimum deposit size and losing new users who start with small test deposits but may later become valuable.

## Selected Achievement Bullets
- Estimated revenue, transaction and user-loss impact of a minimum deposit increase using 389K deposit records.
- Built user segmentation by deposit behavior and analyzed first-deposit category impact on LTV.
- Identified payment methods and traffic sources most exposed to small-deposit loss.

## Relevance Notes
Relevant for pricing, payments, product monetization, user lifecycle and churn-risk analytics.

Exact market, payment methods and currency details are generalized.
