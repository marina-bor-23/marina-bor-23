# Analytics Case Library

This folder is a structured, anonymized library of product and data analytics case studies.

The purpose of this library is to present project experience in a clear and reusable format:

- business context;
- analytical approach;
- metrics and tools;
- business impact;
- anonymized project summaries by domain.

All cases are anonymized: company names, internal product names, specific GEOs, sensitive payment method names and user identifiers are intentionally removed or generalized.

## How to Read

Cases are grouped by analytical domain and role focus:

- Product analytics: funnels, retention, cohorts, LTV, A/B tests, product health.
- Data analytics: SQL/Python pipelines, dashboards, reporting automation, metrics.
- Payments / fintech analytics: deposits, withdrawals, failed transactions, payment method risk.
- CRM / retention analytics: VIP segmentation, reactivation, bonus offers, campaign ROI.
- Growth analytics: registration funnel, activation, traffic-source analysis, campaign tests.

Use `data/cases_catalog.csv` as a compact index of domains, tags, tools and metrics.

## Case Format

Each case includes:

- business question;
- role targets;
- stack;
- metrics;
- what I did;
- business impact;
- selected achievement bullets;
- relevance notes;
- anonymization notes.
